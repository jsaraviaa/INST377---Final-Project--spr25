About Nutrition Intuition

Nutrition Intuition is created for the sole mission to make nutrition tracking accessible, stress-free, and data-driven.

The Problem

Many people struggle to maintain a balanced diet and don’t know where to begin. We wanted to reduce the overwhelm and provide a solution that helps users log meals, set goals, and get trusted nutrition info instantly.

Who We Help

  • People trying to lose or gain weight
  • Health-conscious individuals
  • Nutritionists and dietitians
  • Anyone interested in public health

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Chart.js
  • Backend: Vercel Serverless Functions
  • Database: Supabase
  • API: Spoonacular Nutrition API
